After giving it a lot of thought, I've decided not to create a dedicated section for the old Aznalubma articles (the few that are worth reading). I think the best solution is to absorb everything by assigning the articles to their actual writing date.

This solution allows me to continue writing about the rescue world and to keep the same articles under the same __category__, since their purpose was mostly informative anyway (and quite polemical too, but that's all part of the package).

I'll take this opportunity to sing the praises of static blogs — the ease with which they let me take individual articles and move everything around while keeping it all local is intoxicating.
